Ogilvy Presents...


"BOOV GROOVE"

Objective



Help DreamWorks generate buzz and get fans engaged leading up to the release of their new animated feature "Home," staring Jim Parsons, Rihanna, Jennifer Lopez, and Steve Martin.

Encourage fans to ask for exclusive movie content.


Solution



Using a microsite and Twitter and Facebook, DreamWorks will encouraged fans to Tweet with #BOOVGROOVE in order to unlock Rhainna's original movie soundtracks and exclusive movie trailer content. The more fans Tweet, the closer they come to unlocking content at different thresholds. Once the volume goals are hit, the content unlocks and gives fans access to Rhianna's songs, five pictures and an exclusive video clip.
